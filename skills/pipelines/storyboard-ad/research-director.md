# Research Director - Storyboard Ad Pipeline

## When To Use
First stage. You are grounding a commercial in reality: the product, the audience, the platform norms, and what competing ads actually do.

## Process
1. **Product truth.** Collect verifiable claims only; anything destined for on-screen text needs a source you can cite in the qa_report.
2. **Competitor shot patterns.** Decompose 3-5 reference ads into shot lists (duration, framing, function: hook / demo / social proof / CTA). You are stealing structure, not content.
3. **Platform norms.** Target duration, aspect ratio, caption conventions, loudness targets for the intended placement.

## Output
`research_brief` with: claims + sources, audience notes, 2-3 structural patterns expressed as shot-count/duration skeletons, platform spec sheet.

## Review Bar
Generic filler ("engaging content resonates with users") fails review. Every statement should change a downstream decision.
