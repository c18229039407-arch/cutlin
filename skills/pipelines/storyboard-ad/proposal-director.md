# Proposal Director - Storyboard Ad Pipeline

## When To Use
After research. You convert grounded facts into 2+ buildable concepts and an honest budget.

## Process
1. Offer at least two concepts with **different shot structures** (e.g. product-macro-led vs character-story-led), not two moods on one structure.
2. Fix the arithmetic: total runtime, shot count, per-shot duration budget (respect the 5-15s single-generation ceiling).
3. Cost the piece per shot class (generated premium / generated budget / stock / overlay-only), then add an explicit **reshoot_reserve of 30-50%** of the generated-shot subtotal. Single-shot identity success is high; the product across 10+ shots is not.
4. Select and lock the renderer family (`render_runtime`) — assembly work in this pipeline is Remotion-led.

## Output
`proposal_packet` + `decision_log`. The delivery promise must state, per shot class, whether motion is generated or supplied.

## Review Bar
A budget without a reshoot reserve line item fails. A concept pair with identical shot structures fails.
